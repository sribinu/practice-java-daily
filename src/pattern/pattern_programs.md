# Pattern Program Questions

## Star Patterns

### 1. Filled Square
Print a filled square pattern of '*' of size N × N.
```
* * * * * 
* * * * * 
* * * * * 
* * * * * 
* * * * * 
```

### 2. Hollow Square
Print a hollow square of '*' of size N × N.
```
* * * * * 
*       * 
*       * 
*       * 
* * * * * 
```

### 3. Plus
Print a plus pattern using '*' of size N (odd N).
```
Enter an odd number N: 5
    *     
    *     
* * * * * 
    *     
    *     
```
